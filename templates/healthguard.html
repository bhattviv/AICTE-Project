Health Tips & Precautions

Follow these health tips and precautions to help prevent and recover from diseases.

Diabetes - Tips & Precautions

  • Maintain a balanced diet with plenty of fiber and low sugar.
  • Engage in regular physical activity like walking or cycling.
  • Monitor blood glucose levels regularly as advised by your doctor.
  • Avoid alcohol and smoking, as they can worsen the condition.
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques like yoga or meditation.

Heart Disease - Tips & Precautions

  • Follow a heart-healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Limit salt, fat, and cholesterol intake to maintain healthy blood pressure.
  • Exercise regularly, aiming for at least 30 minutes of moderate activity most days of the week.
  • Quit smoking and avoid exposure to second-hand smoke.
  • Take prescribed medications as directed by your healthcare provider.

General Disease Prevention Tips

  • Wash your hands frequently to prevent the spread of infections.
  • Get vaccinated against common diseases like flu, pneumonia, and other preventable conditions.
  • Maintain a healthy weight and stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Ensure adequate sleep and manage stress effectively.
  • Practice safe hygiene, especially when preparing and handling food.
